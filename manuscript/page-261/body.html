Silently we went on and apart. When I came home, it was day become.

* * * * *

The best thing that brought me that time in St. was with me for hours Pistorius on the organ or in front of the fireplace. We read one Greek text about Abraxas together, he read me pieces one Translation from the Vedas and taught me to speak the holy "Om". Yet it was not these scholarships that kept me at home promoted, but rather the opposite. What was good for me was that Forward find in myself, the increasing confidence in my own Dreams, thoughts and hunches, and the increasing knowledge of the power that I carried in me.
