"So you wanted to kill yourself, Knauer?"

He shuddered with cold and fear.

"Yes I wanted. I do not know if I could have done it. I wanted wait until it is morning. "

I pulled him outside. The first horizontal light stripes of the day glowed unspeakably cold and listless in the gray airs.

I led the boy a long way on the arm. It said to me, "Now Do you go home and do not tell anyone! You are the wrong way gone, the wrong way! We are not pigs, as you think. We are human beings. We make gods and fight with them and bless them us."
