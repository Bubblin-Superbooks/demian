"I have nothing," I said sadly. "I have no money. But otherwise I want to give you everything. I have an Indian book, and soldiers, and a compass. I want to get it to you. "

Kromer just shrugged his bold, evil mouth and spat on the floor.

"Do not talk!" He said commandingly. "You can do your rag stuff to keep. A compass! Do not make me angry anymore, you hear, and give the money! "

"But I do not have any, I never get any money. It's not my fault!"

"So you'll bring me the two marks tomorrow. I'm waiting for school down at the market. Finished. If you do not bring any money, you will see!"
