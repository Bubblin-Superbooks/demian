He smiled and took the watch in his big hand. I looked at that hand and felt how rough and deeply hostile she was to me, as she was after mine Life and peace reached.

"She's made of silver -" I said shyly.

"I'm going to whistle for your silver and your old watch!" He said lower Contempt. "Just let them fix it yourself!"

"But Franz," I exclaimed, shaking with fear, he wanted to run away. "Wait a little! Take the watch! She is really silver, really and true. And I have nothing else. "

He looked at me coolly and contemptuously.
