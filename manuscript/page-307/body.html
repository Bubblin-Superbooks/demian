"As long as the dream is your destiny, you should remain faithful to it," she confirmed seriously.

A sadness seized me, and the longing, in this enchanted hour to die. I felt the tears - how infinite I had not cried for a long time! - irresistibly swelling in me and overwhelm me. I turned away from her, stepped to the window and looked over the potted flowers with blind eyes.

Behind me, I heard her voice, she sounded calm and yet was so full of tenderness like a cup filled to the brim with wine.
