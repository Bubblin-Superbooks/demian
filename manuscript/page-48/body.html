I hid the rifle in the lower hallway, kept the money in the closed hand and stepped out of the house, unlike me ever by this Goal had gone. Above someone shouted for me, it seemed to me; I walked fast of it.

There was still a lot of time, I made a detour through the streets of one changed city, under unseen clouds, past houses, the looking at me and at people who suspected me. On the way fell to me that a schoolmate of mine once on the cattle market a thaler had found. I would like to pray that God will do a miracle and me may also make such a find. But I was no longer right to pray. And even then the rifle would not have become whole again.
