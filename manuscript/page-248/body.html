"You are abstemious too?" He asked anxiously.

"How do you mean? Do you mean the sexual? "

"Yes / Yes. I've been abstaining for two years now, since I was apprenticed White. Before, I drove a vice, you know. -- So you are never been with a woman? "

"No," I said. "I did not find the right one."

"But if you find the one you think is the right one, then would you sleep with her? "

"Yes of course. - If she does not mind, "I said with some mockery.

"Oh, there you are on the wrong path! The internal forces can only be train, if you stay completely abstemious. I did it, two years long. Two years and just over a month! It is so hard! Sometimes I can hardly stand it anymore. "
